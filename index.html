Frontend.vlaanderen

About us

The PHP community in the region is a very active one. There are many active user groups that organize meetups on a regular basis. Frontend developers - aka people with a general interest in HTML, CSS and Javascript - seem to lack such an official commmunity.

Frontend.Vlaanderen is a brand new initiative trying to unite the flemish webdesigner community by organizing meetups including frontend-related workshops and talks.

Hopefully, we will organize our first meetup around Q2 2015.

So stay tuned and keep an eye on our Twitter account! In the meanwhile, join us in our very own public HipChat rooms.

Can I join ?

There is no offical/legal organization (yet) and there is no active membership registration either. The only thing you can do for now is follow us on Twitter

If you wish to organize a meetup at your company or perhaps talk at another host company, feel free to send an e-mail.
